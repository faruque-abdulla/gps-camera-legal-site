Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GPS Camera ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the mobile application.

GPS Camera lets you capture photos with visible location information such as coordinates, address details, and timestamps. This policy is intended to satisfy Google Play privacy policy requirements and help users understand what data the app accesses, why it is needed, and how it is handled.

1. Information the App Accesses

  • Camera: used only to let you capture photos inside the app.
  • Location: used to obtain your device location so the app can show coordinates, address details, and other location-based information on the photo.
  • Storage or media: used to save generated photos to your device gallery and to access app-created image files needed for app functionality.
  • Photos you create in the app: used to generate previews, apply overlays, and save the final image when you choose to do so.

2. How Information Is Used

  • To capture photos requested by you.
  • To add GPS coordinates, address details, and timestamps onto your photos.
  • To generate map or address information associated with the captured location.
  • To save the final processed photo locally on your device when you tap save.

3. Permissions Used by the App

The app requests only the permissions needed for its current features.

  • Camera permission: required to take photos within the app.
  • Location permission: required to fetch precise or approximate location so the app can add location information to photos.
  • Media or storage permission: required to save photos you create and, where applicable, access related image files needed by the app.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

  • The app does not require account signup.
  • The app does not sell your personal data.
  • The app does not upload your photos to our own server.
  • If reverse geocoding, map preview, or address lookup features are used, latitude and longitude data may be sent to third-party mapping or geocoding providers solely to return location details such as address, locality, or map content.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • Photos you save are stored on your device.
  • Temporary files may be created locally while the app processes captured photos.
  • The app does not provide its own cloud storage for your photos.
  • We do not retain your photos, location history, or personal account information on our own servers because the app does not currently operate a user account system or developer-hosted backend for this purpose.

6. User Control

  • You can deny permissions, but some core features may not function without them.
  • You can manage or revoke permissions later from your Android device settings.
  • You can delete saved photos from your device whenever you want.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to limit access to data to what is necessary for the app's core functionality. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. International Transfers

If a third-party mapping or geocoding provider processes location data to return address details, that processing may occur on systems located outside your country, subject to that provider's own terms and privacy practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy over time. Updated versions will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
